The Witcher 3

Watch Trailer

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a 2015 action role-playing game developed and published by CD Projekt.

It is the sequel to the 2011 game The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings and the third game in The Witcher video game series, played in an open world with a third-person perspective.

The games are based on The Witcher series of fantasy novels written by Andrzej Sapkowski.
